Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my Favorite food. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich and scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt a butter in frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once a butter has melted add a cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if ypu are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate untill it served).
